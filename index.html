About

Over two decades building products that bring impact for business, value for customers, and joy for users, including 11 years at Google.

As a Google product manager, I shipped multilingual, global, multi-platform products. I learned to craft product vision and strategy, run product discovery, and plan with measurable outcomes.

Beyond Google, at Lyft, Kaia Health, Taktile, and Munich Re, I learned to establish impact-driven practices across radically different contexts: from startup to enterprise, from autonomous vehicles to healthcare AI.
